# MCI Eyelashes — Product & Marketing Context

## Company Overview

**MCI Eyelashes** is a factory-direct B2B wholesale eyelash manufacturer headquartered in Purbalingga, Central Java, Indonesia. The company supplies premium handcrafted eyelashes to salons, distributors, and beauty brands across 50+ countries, with private label and OEM services available.

- **Founded:** ~2015 (10+ years in business)
- **Scale:** 500+ global partners, 10M+ units shipped
- **Certifications:** ISO 9001, CE certified, FDA compliant, PETA approved
- **Contact:** Denis — denis@mci-eyelashes.com
- **Response SLA:** 24 hours on all inquiries

## Brand Positioning & Messaging

**Elevator Pitch:** Factory-direct wholesale lash manufacturer with handcrafted quality, flexible MOQ, and global shipping — no middleman markup.

**Core Value Props:**
1. **Precision Craft** — Every lash placed and inspected by artisans with 10+ years of experience
2. **Premium Materials** — Certified, cruelty-free fibers from trusted global suppliers
3. **Scalable Supply** — From boutique orders to full container loads
4. **Global Reach** — Insured international shipping to 50+ countries with full tracking

**Key Differentiators vs. competitors:**
- Factory-direct (eliminates distributor margin)
- ISO 9001 + CE + FDA + PETA certifications
- Fast production: 3–7 days standard lead time
- Worldwide shipping: 5–14 days
- Flexible starting MOQ: 30–50 units per style (volume), 100 pairs standard
- Transparent 7-point quality control inspection
- Educational content and 24-hour support

---

## Manufacturing Process

1. **Premium Sourcing** — Certified, cruelty-free suppliers; incoming material QC
2. **Expert Crafting** — Skilled artisans, 10+ years experience, handcrafted production
3. **Quality Control** — 7-point inspection: material integrity, curl consistency, length accuracy, hygiene standards, packaging quality
4. **Global Delivery** — Insured shipping with full tracking; pristine packaging on arrival
